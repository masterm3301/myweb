<!---->

This system was created by a group of highly analytical thinkers working with early-stage startups, shaping rules aligned with their culture and an environment of capital abundance.<!---->

    <h2 id="ember64" class="ember-view reader-text-block__heading-2">
      <!---->The Startup Lifecycle<!---->
<!---->    </h2>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember65" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">
      <!---->Before discussing funding rounds, it is important to understand the lifecycle of a startup. I will divide it into more stages than usual, because details matter.<!---->
<!---->    </p>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <h3 id="ember66" class="ember-view reader-text-block__heading-3">
      <!---->1. Ideation Stage<!---->
<!---->    </h3>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember67" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">
      <!---->Founders explore ideas and validate the Proof of Concept (PoC), ensuring the idea is technically feasible.<!---->
<!---->    </p>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <h3 id="ember68" class="ember-view reader-text-block__heading-3">
      <!---->2. MVP &amp; Early Validation<!---->
<!---->    </h3>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember69" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">
      <!---->Instead of building a full product, startups focus on a minimal version. The goal is not profit, but data and learning.<!---->
<!---->    </p>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <h3 id="ember70" class="ember-view reader-text-block__heading-3">
      <!---->3. Product-Market Fit (PMF)<!---->
<!---->    </h3>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember71" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">
      <!---->This is the most critical and difficult stage. Most startups fail here. Continuous iteration—or even a full pivot—is required to match market needs.<!---->
<!---->    </p>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <h3 id="ember72" class="ember-view reader-text-block__heading-3">
      <!---->4. Operational Excellence<!---->
<!---->    </h3>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember73" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">
      <!---->The company evolves from a small team into structured departments and systems, supported by automation.<!---->
<!---->    </p>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <h3 id="ember74" class="ember-view reader-text-block__heading-3">
      <!---->5. Blitzscaling<!---->
<!---->    </h3>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember75" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">
      <!---->Rapid growth becomes the priority, even at the expense of efficiency, to dominate the market.<!---->
<!---->    </p>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember77" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">

<!---->

<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember96" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">
            <ul><li><!---->Funding: $5k – $10k<!----></li><li><!---->Duration: up to 3 months<span class="white-space-pre"> </span></li><li><!---->Mechanism: self-funding or grants<!----></li><li><!---->Dilution: 0%<!----></li></ul>

<!---->    </p>

<!---->

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember98" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">

<!---->

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember99" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">
            <ul><li><!---->Funding: $5k – $15k<!----></li><li><!---->Duration: 2–5 months<!----></li><li><!---->Mechanism: SAFE (no cap preferred)<!----></li><li><!---->Dilution: 1% – 3%<!----></li></ul>

<!---->    </p>

  

<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->
<!---->            
        
    <p id="ember100" class="ember-view reader-text-block__paragraph">
